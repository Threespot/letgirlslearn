The Countries

The Peace Corps’ Let Girls Learn Program operates in 35 countries worldwide to make lasting impact, expanding into these countries in the last year: Armenia, Cameroon, Costa Rica, Ecuador, Fiji, The Gambia, Guinea, Guyana, Jamaica, Lesotho, Liberia, Madagascar, Malawi, Panama, Peru, Samoa, Senegal, Sierra Leone, South Africa, Tanzania, Vanuatu and Zambia.

During the first year of the initiative, Peace Corps worked in 13 target countries, including: Albania, Benin, Burkina Faso, Cambodia, Ethiopia, Georgia, Ghana, Moldova, Mongolia, Mozambique, Thailand, Togo, and Uganda.
